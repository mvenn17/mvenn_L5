My Halloween Costume

For Halloween this year, my boyfriend and I dressed up as Wirt and Greg From Over the Garden Wall. We made our costumes ourselves, with a little help from a friend on some of the sewing. Our costumes are a mix of handmade, 3D printed, and purchased items.

Over the Garden Wall is a show about two half-brothers who travel through a mysterious forest to find their way home. Througout their journey, they encounter a variety of strange things. It was released in November of 2014.
